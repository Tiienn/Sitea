03 — Market Gaps

What Nobody Does Well

These are the underserved areas across all competitors that represent real opportunities for differentiation.

01
Land-first design flow

No consumer tool starts with "what's your land size?" then helps you design what fits. Cedreo does terrain for pros at a high price. Everyone else starts with an empty room. Homeowners buying land have no tool.

02
Outdoor structures in one tool

Pools, fences, foundations, and stairs are typically separate apps or premium add-ons. Home Design 3D sells a separate "Outdoor & Garden" app. Planner 5D has limited outdoor support. Nobody bundles them free.

03
Truly mobile-first building

Competitors have mobile apps but they're stripped-down versions of desktop. Touch controls are afterthoughts. No one has purpose-built joystick + camera look controls for phone-first 3D building.

04
Zero-friction onboarding

Most competitors require signup before doing anything. Planner 5D limits free to 2 projects. RoomSketcher charges $38/plan. There's demand for a tool that works immediately, no account needed.

05
Simple template-based building

User complaints across competitors: too complex, steep learning curve, overwhelming UI. There's a gap for "pick a template, customize it" approach instead of "here's a blank canvas, figure it out."

06
Real-time 3D with no render wait

Most competitors separate "editing" from "viewing." You design in 2D, then click "render" and wait. Instant first-person walkthrough while building is rare. Only Arcadium competes here.

05 — Recommendations

Strategic Next Moves

Based on market analysis, these are the highest-impact moves ranked by effort vs. differentiation value.

P1
Double down on the land-first story

This is Sitea's clearest differentiator. Enhance the onboarding to make "what fits on my land?" the hero experience. Add setback line visualization, lot coverage calculations, and building-to-land ratio indicators.

P2
Add basic furniture placement

Every competitor has this. Even a small library (bed, sofa, table, chair, kitchen counter) would close the biggest feature gap and let users fully visualize their space.

P3
Export shareable links & screenshots

Users need to share designs with family, contractors, or on social media. A screenshot/share feature is low effort and high virality. This is how Planner 5D grew to 120M users.

P4
AI floor plan generation

Given land dimensions and preferences (bedrooms, bathrooms, open plan), generate a layout automatically. This would be a massive differentiator: "Tell us your land size, we'll design your house."
